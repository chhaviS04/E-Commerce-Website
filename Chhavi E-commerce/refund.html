Refund Policy

1. Overview - At Chhavi E-commerce, we are committed to ensuring your satisfaction with your purchase. If you are not satisfied with your order, we are here to help. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are issued, the process for requesting a refund, and other relevant information.


2.Eligibility for Refunds- You may be eligible for a refund under the following conditions: The item is returned within 30 days from the date of purchase. The item is unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as when you received it. Proof of purchase (receipt or order number) is provided.


3. Non-Refundable Items- Certain items are non-refundable. These include: Downloadable software products, Gift cards, Items purchased on sale or with a discount Perishable goods such as food, flowers, or magazines


4.Refund Process- To initiate a refund, please follow these steps: Contact Us: Email our customer support team at [customer.support@yourcompany.com] or call us at [Customer Support Phone Number]. Provide your order number and reason for the refund request. Return Authorization: Our team will review your request and, if eligible, provide you with a Return Authorization (RA) number and return instructions. Ship the Item: Package the item securely, include the proof of purchase and RA number, and ship it to the address provided by our customer support team. You are responsible for the return shipping costs. Inspection and Approval:Once we receive the returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.


5. Refunds (if applicable) Approved Refunds: If your refund is approved, we will process it within 7-10 business days. The refund will be applied to your original method of payment. Partial Refunds: Partial refunds may be granted for items that are not in their original condition, are damaged, or are missing parts for reasons not due to our error. Late or Missing Refunds: If you have not received your refund within the stated timeframe, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company and bank, as it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. If you still have not received your refund, please contact us at [customer.support@yourcompany.com].
